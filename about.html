About Me

Gary is a UX/UI desginer with a background in psychology. He aims to design intuitive UI that provide delightful user experience.

I am a UX/UI desginer with a background in psychology. Who love learning about human behaviour, cognition and perception. I want to create captivating UI that are backed by thorough UX research.

Not long ago, I decided to sign up for a UX/UI boot camp that was provided by Trilogy and Adelaide University. I expected to learn more about web and app design, in hope of one day I will be able to turn my ideas into real products. However, unexpectedly, I fall in love with the UX process, and since then, I have tried to grow myself into a better UX researcher.

Where possible, I try to utilise my academic researching and analysing skills from my day of studying psychology in Adelaide University for my UX/UI projects. My understanding of human behaviour, perception and cognition also provide me the insights that I need to become a better UX/UI designer.

I also have an interest in pop culture in general and Japanese pop culture specifically. I have been podcasting with my mates since 2015, basically using that as an excuse to talk about our favourite franchises.
